Our Story

We're Building the Operating System for Modern Business

Every business owner we talked to had the same problem: too many tools, not enough clarity. They had one app for sales, another for stock, a spreadsheet for expenses, and a notebook for debts. Nothing talked to each other. Decisions were made on gut feeling because the data was scattered everywhere.

We built FaidaHQ to fix that. One platform where every part of your business lives — your sales, your stock, your books, your customers, your team, and even your social media. You open FaidaHQ and you know exactly where your business stands. No more guesswork. No more switching between five apps. No more end-of-month surprises.

We built it to work for any business — from a corner shop to a growing logistics startup. And we built it to work even without a reliable internet connection, because not every business runs in a city with fast Wi-Fi.
